Detailing Services

PROFESSIONAL
DETAILING
SERVICES

What is car detailing? Car detailing is a process of deep cleaning and restoring a vehicle's interior and exterior surfaces. It uses specialized tools, chemicals, and techniques beyond regular washing.

What is included in interior detailing? Interior detailing includes vacuuming, stain removal, dashboard cleaning, and surface conditioning. Materials such as fabric, leather, and plastic are treated using appropriate cleaning agents.

What is included in exterior detailing? Exterior detailing includes washing, polishing, paint correction, and protective coating. This improves surface clarity and protects paint from environmental damage.

How long does detailing take? Time varies depending on vehicle size, condition, and service level. Basic detailing takes a few hours, while full correction and coating require extended time.

Performance Upgrades

PERFORMANCE
UPGRADES
& TUNING

What are performance upgrades? Performance upgrades are modifications that improve engine power, acceleration, and efficiency. These changes involve mechanical components and electronic tuning systems.

What is ECU tuning? ECU tuning adjusts engine control parameters such as fuel injection and ignition timing. This improves engine response and efficiency when done correctly within safe limits.

What are common performance parts? Common parts include exhaust systems, air intake systems, turbo components, and fuel system upgrades. Compatibility depends on vehicle model and engine configuration.

Are performance upgrades safe? Upgrades are safe when installed correctly and matched to the engine's capacity. Incorrect tuning or incompatible parts can reduce reliability.
